My Hobbies

When I'm not busy with surgeries and sailing the Grand Line, here are a few things I enjoy:

  • New Stuff: I like reading about medicine even when I'm not in the hospital. It's not just about fixing people; I enjoy learning new things in the medical world.

  • Sword Practice: I've got this cool sword named "Kikoku." It's not just for fights; it's like a form of exercise and expression for me.

  • Exploring New Islands: As a pirate, I love finding new islands. It's not always about battles; I enjoy checking out different places and meeting new people.

  • Listening to Music: Music is my escape. Whether it's a calm tune during quiet times or the lively beats from different islands, it helps me relax and get inspired.

  • Playing Chess: I like playing strategic games like chess. It's not just about surgery; I enjoy thinking ahead and planning my moves.
